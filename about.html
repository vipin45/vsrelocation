About Us

Vs-relocation is a fully licensed moving company operative since year 2020. The company provide fast, reliable and economical moving services in India. We at Vs-relocation have worked hard to build a solid reputation with our customers. We are fully licensed and insured Packers and Movers and we are proud that they have come to depend on us for our exceptional level of quality service at affordable prices. Our main goal at Vs-relocation is to provide our valued customers with the very best moving services available at the most affordable price possible. Our clients feel reassured and confident that all of us here at Vs-relocation will go that extra mile to treat their treasured personal belongings as if they were our own. From planning the move, to packing, transportation and storage we will do everything we can to ensure our customers are complete satisfaction with our services. It has been our continued priority to listen carefully to our customers, so that we can personalize our moving services and tailor them to specifically meet each customers individual needs and requirements. The very foundation of our business is built on our guarantee to ensure that any service provided by Vs-relocation - be it residential or commercial moving, storage, or anything else - is completed to the total satisfaction and approval of our valued customers. Our price is very competitive as compared to other Packers and Movers in India. Our service area covers entire India. Additionally, all of our worker are rigorously trained in up to date safety procedures/protocol to ensure your safety as well as their own while providing any and all of the moving services offered by Vs-relocation.
